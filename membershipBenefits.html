Why Join The Chamber?

Networking Opportunities

Joining a Chamber of a manageable size gives members the opportunity to make real connections and establish stronger relationships with other organizations and business leaders.

Monthly Lunch & Learn Events

A time where members gather to network and listen to dynamic speakers who bring a variety of key subjects to the table, including business trends and know-how for the area’s small and mid size businesses.

Community

The South Orange Chamber of Commerce is a civic not-for-profit organization of citizens, businesses and professional people who provide leadership skills, share their resources, experience and talents, providing the framework to build a strong and healthy community.

Quartely Business After Hours (BAH)

These events are held in the early evening and offer a more relaxed atmosphere to socialize and network with other members, their guests and partnering organizations.

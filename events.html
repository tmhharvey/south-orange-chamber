Chamber Events

Monthly Lunch & Learn Seminars

A time where members gather to network and listen to dynamic speakers who bring a variety of key subjects to the table, including business trends and know-how for the area’s small and mid size businesses.

Chamber Members can win the Member Spotlight at each Lunch and Learn with the Spotlight on the winner’s business listed in the 'South Orange Connections' newsletter. Contact the Chamber if you are interested in sponsoring or presenting at one of the Lunch and Learn seminars.


Quarterly Business After Hours

These events are held in the early evening and offer a more relaxed atmosphere to socialize and network with other members, their guests and partnering organizations.

A chamber member may host a BAH at their place of business as a way of showcasing what they do and who they are. BAH are free to all chamber members and a small fee is asked for guests and non-partnering groups. Contact the chamber if you are interested in hosting a BAH.
